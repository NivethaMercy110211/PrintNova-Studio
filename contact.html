Prepress Guide

Artwork Preparation Checklist

Before submitting your inquiry or file, run through these checkpoints for a smooth prepress review.

1. Resolution (300 DPI)
Ensure all raster elements and images embedded in your files are saved at a minimum resolution of 300 DPI at final print output dimensions to prevent pixelation.
2. Color Profile (CMYK)
Set your color palette workspace to CMYK (preferably Coated FOGRA39 profile) rather than RGB, preventing shifting print color tones during conversion.
3. Convert to Outlines
Convert all typography layers and vector texts to outlines or embeds. This removes dependencies on external custom fonts when opening on prepress tools.
4. Bleed & Safe Margins
Include a 3mm outer bleed margin for cutting tolerance and position critical logo branding components at least 4mm inside standard page boundaries.
