Our Story

Built on Passion for Print & Brand Quality

PrintNova Studio was founded in 2018 with a simple mission — make professional-grade printing accessible to every business, regardless of size. What started as a small digital print setup has grown into a full-service online printing powerhouse.

Today, we operate state-of-the-art presses across three facilities in South India, serving clients from startups to Fortune 500 companies with consistent, high-quality output.

Production Process

From Upload to Delivery — How We Work

1
Order Placement
Customer selects product, uploads artwork, configures print specs, and places order through our secure platform.
2
Preflight & Proofing
Our prepress team checks artwork for print-readiness and generates a digital proof for customer approval.
3
Press Production
Approved jobs go to our production floor where offset or digital presses run the job with precision colour management.
4
Finishing
Lamination, UV coating, cutting, folding, binding — all finishing operations done in-house for precision and speed.
5
Quality Control
Every batch is inspected for colour accuracy, registration, and print defects before being approved for packing.
6
Pack & Dispatch
Securely packed and handed to our logistics partner for same-day, express or standard delivery to your location.
