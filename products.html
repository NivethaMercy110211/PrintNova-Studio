Material Specs

Substrate & Paper Quality Guide

Choose the perfect substrate weight and texture for your brand print jobs.

300gsm Art Card
Thick, rigid, and premium. Best for high-end business cards, presentation folders, and certificates. Available in matte or gloss.
130gsm/170gsm Art Paper
Lightweight and highly reflective. Ideal for multi-page booklets, catalogues, brochures, and flyers that require vibrant colour.
Rustic Kraft Paper
100% recycled unbleached brown paper. Delivers a tactile, natural, and eco-friendly texture perfect for artisanal packaging.
Weatherproof Flex
Heavy-duty 440gsm reinforced flex fabric. Completely waterproof and UV fade-resistant, designed for outdoor signage.
