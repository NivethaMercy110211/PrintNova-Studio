Eco-Commitment

Green Printing Initiatives

We balance high-fidelity print quality with deep ecological responsibility in our production workflows.

Soy-Based Printing Inks
We substitute traditional petroleum inks with organic, soy-based alternatives. This cuts VOC emissions, improves cardboard recyclability, and outputs brighter, richer color yields.
FSC-Certified Stock Materials
We offer FSC-certified recycled paper and board options across our stationery lines, ensuring the raw pulps are sourced exclusively from responsibly managed forestry.
Chemical-Free Prepress
Our digital plate production utilizes chemical-free imaging. This eliminates the disposal of heavy developers and chemical toxins into the local water networks.
